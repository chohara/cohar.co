Yoshi's Crafted World

Yoshi's Crafted World is a standard Yoshi game at it's core. Unfortunately, though, Crafted World seems to be poorly crafted right from the start, and hardly improves as the game goes on. The gameplay is simple, and reminds of a DS game. The music is forgettable - a rare trait for a title in the Yoshi series. All in all, Crafted World is an acceptable platformer, but nothing remarkable.

Play it for the:

  • Cute characters
  • Relaxing platforming
  • Love of Yoshi
