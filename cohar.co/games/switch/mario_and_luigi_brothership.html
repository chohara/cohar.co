Mario & Luigi: Brothership

Before picking up Mario & Luigi: Brothership, I had only ever played Superstar Saga, for the GBA. Going in, I didn't really know what to expect - I was drawn in by the cute and unique look for the titular characters. What I got was a fun and charming JRPG, with a creative story and some genuinely funny dialogue. Mario & Luigi: Brothership is a JRPG on easy-mode, yet it manages to be complex enough to remain engaging. Ultimately, it delivers a fun and memorable journey that's bursting with unique charm.

Play it for the:

  • Joyful level design
  • Mellow JRPG gameplay
  • Cartoonish art and animations
