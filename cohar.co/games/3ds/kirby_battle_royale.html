Kirby Battle Royale

Kirby Battle Royale isn't an actual game in the battle royale genre - it instead plays as a series of minigames intended for four player multiplayer. For the sake of this review, I only completed the story mode without really doing multiplayer - since I'm not friends with any losers who play mediocre old Kirby games. Kirby Battle Royale is ultimately a disappointment, with barely enough charm to make it palatable. With there only being ten different minigames, I found myself bored with nearly all of them by the end of the short story mode. With that considered, I don't doubt that Kirby Battle Royale would be a lot more fun with friends.

Play it for the:

  • Lovable Kirby cast
  • Fun remixes of classic Kirby tunes
  • Multiplayer, probably
