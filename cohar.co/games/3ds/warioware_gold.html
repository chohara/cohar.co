WarioWare Gold

WarioWare Gold is much like the other titles in the series, but with extra polish. In a lot of ways, Gold feels like the culmination of handheld WarioWare - combining the old with the new. It adds some additional modes, which provide a lot of new ways to play, even an online mode! While it doesn't reinvent the formula and remains quite short, WarioWare Gold feels like a more worthwhile investment than the previous handheld installments in the series. I found the final stage in particular to be a lot fun, mixing a variety of minigame types and providing a greater challenge than usual. Some of the Touched minigames really brought back some memories, and that token WarioWare weirdness is availble in droves - I'm glad to have played it!

Play it for the:

  • Friedness
  • Culmination of handheld WarioWare
  • Challenge modes
