WarioWare, Inc.: Mega Microgame$!

The WarioWare series is characterized by the presence of "microgames", which are extremely short minigames - merely three or so seconds each. WarioWare, Inc.: Mega Microgame$! - yes, that is the official title - is the first entry in the series. Given that it was limited to GBA hardware, Mega Microgame$! is exceptionally simple and never becomes particularly challenging. It succeeds stylistically, and sets the tone for the series to come. The characters, setting and sound are all quirky - which I find endearing. WarioWare, Inc.: Mega Microgame$! is a solid introduction to the series, but it has not aged gracefully.

Play it for the:

  • History of WarioWare
  • Fried fun
  • Classic GBA vibe
