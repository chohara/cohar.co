Iridion 3D

Iridion 3D was a launch title for the Game Boy Advance, meant to showcase the power of the device. In all honesty, I only felt obligated to play this game because the sequel - Iridion II - has a good reputation, and I plan on playing it. Iridion 3D definitely succeeds in showing off the visual capabilities of the GBA, as you fly a ship through a makeshift 3D space (it plays like a rail shooter). The gameplay is fun at it's core, but is quite frustrating due to problems deciphering depth. The bosses were easily a highlight for me, with fun patterns and intense tunes. I look forward to giving the sequel a try - this one was fun, but definitely has its flaws.

Play it for the:

  • Impressive "3D" visuals
  • Fun and challenging boss fights
  • Alien blasting action
