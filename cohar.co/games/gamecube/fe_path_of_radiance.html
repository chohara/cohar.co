Fire Emblem: Path of Radiance

Fire Emblem: Path of Radiance is a solid entry point into the series and the continent of Tellius. The story leans into morally complex territory, with characters whose motivations are rarely black and white, and a plot that doesn't shy away from difficult themes. The gameplay is streamlined compared to later entries - permadeath is present, but the overall difficulty is forgiving enough to let you focus on the story and characters.

Play it for the:

  • Morally ambiguous characters and plot
  • Easy and simplified gameplay
  • Introduction to the continent of Tellius
