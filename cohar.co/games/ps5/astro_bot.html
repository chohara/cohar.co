Astro Bot

Astro Bot is a follow-up to the lauded tech demo for the PS5, Astro's Playroom, and it picks up right where Playroom left off. Astro Bot is a premium 3D platformer - with a vibrant world, tight controls, and a fun atmosphere that celebrates PlayStation's history. Each world is so unique, and calls back to an iconic setting in PlayStation history - before you know it, you're rushing to see what the next stage will be. This is Sony's Mario, and so far Astro Bot is doing a pretty good impression.

Play it for the:

  • Addicting 3D platforming
  • Throwback characters and worlds
  • Flawless controls and integration
