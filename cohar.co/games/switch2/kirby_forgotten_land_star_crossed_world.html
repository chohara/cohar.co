Kirby and the Forgotten Land + Star Crossed World

I had a great time with Kirby and the Forgotten Land when it originally launched, so I was excited to revisit it with the Star Crossed World upgrades. On top of the standard Switch 2 suite of changes (60FPS, etc) Star Crossed World offers 12 total additional levels and a few new Mouthful Modes. These new levels are awesome, and recapture the essence that made Forgotten Land so great - tasking you with finding collectables and secrets as usual. The new Mouthful Modes are great, with the gear and sign-sled being my favorites. In addition to the added levels, Star Crossed World introduces a new cup in the colosseum, providing some post-game challenge. This new content is a great continuation of an already great game, use it as an excuse to play this stellar Kirby title one more time.

Play it for the:

  • Continuation of a magnificent Kirby game
  • Challenging new colosseum content
  • Starry and whimsical new levels and characters
